About us

Our Institute

Kavayitri Bahinabai Chaudhari North Maharashtra University, Jalgaon – formerly known as North Maharashtra University – is one of the premier academic institutions in the state of Maharashtra. Established on 15th August 1990, the university has played a pivotal role in shaping the educational landscape of the region. Named in honor of the renowned poetess Kavayitri Bahinabai Chaudhari, the university carries forward her legacy of wisdom, simplicity, and cultural depth. Our institution is committed to fostering academic excellence, research innovation, and inclusive development.

Spread across a serene and eco-friendly campus of 660 acres, the university is located in the historical city of Jalgaon, in the north-western region of Maharashtra. The campus provides an ideal atmosphere for academic pursuits with well-equipped laboratories, advanced research centers, a central library, digital learning facilities, hostels, sports amenities, and green open spaces.

Academic Research

KBCNMU has jurisdiction over three districts – Jalgaon, Dhule, and Nandurbar – and serves a large student population through over 220 affiliated colleges and institutes. We offer a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, doctoral, and diploma programs across disciplines including Science, Engineering, Commerce, Management, Arts, Education, Pharmacy, and Law.

Our Vision & Mission

Our vision is to empower students through inclusive, value-based, and transformative education, nurturing leadership, innovation, and social responsibility. We strive to create an environment where knowledge meets purpose and students are inspired to become global citizens with strong ethical foundations.

Innovation and Developement

KBCNMU encourages a spirit of inquiry and innovation. We support startups, entrepreneurship, and applied research through dedicated incubation centers, MoUs with industry, and skill development initiatives aligned with the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020

Meet Teachers

At Kavayitri Bahinabai Chaudhari North Maharashtra University, our teachers are more than just educators — they are mentors, researchers, and lifelong learners who are passionate about shaping the minds of tomorrow. Our dedicated faculty members bring a wealth of academic expertise, practical experience, and a deep commitment to student success. They foster a learning environment that encourages curiosity, critical thinking, and innovation. Whether delivering lectures, guiding research, or mentoring students, our teachers strive to instill values, knowledge, and a global perspective. Many of them are recognized for their contributions to research, publications, and academic development on national and international platforms. With a student-centric approach and a vision aligned with the university’s mission, our teachers play a vital role in transforming education into a meaningful journey.
