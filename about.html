About Our School

Exploring the beauty and power of mathematics since 1992

Our Story

The School of Mathematical Sciences at North Maharashtra University was established in 1992 with a vision to become a center of excellence in mathematical education and research. Over the past three decades, we have grown into one of the premier mathematics departments in the region.

Our school is dedicated to advancing mathematical knowledge through rigorous academic programs, innovative research, and community engagement. We foster an environment where students can explore the fascinating world of mathematics and develop the analytical skills needed for success in various fields.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional education in mathematical sciences that empowers students with analytical thinking, problem-solving abilities, and mathematical literacy required for success in academia, industry, and society.

We are committed to advancing mathematical knowledge through innovative research and fostering a collaborative environment that promotes intellectual curiosity and academic excellence.

Our Vision

To be recognized nationally and internationally as a center of excellence in mathematical education, research, and innovation that addresses complex challenges and contributes to scientific advancement.

We aspire to produce graduates who are not only proficient in mathematical concepts but also capable of applying these principles to solve real-world problems across various disciplines.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our approach to education, research, and community engagement.

Academic Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in teaching, learning, and research to foster intellectual growth and innovation.

Collaboration

We believe in the power of working together across disciplines to solve complex problems and advance knowledge.

Innovation

We encourage creative thinking and novel approaches to mathematical problems and educational methods.

Inclusivity

We value diversity and create an environment where all students can thrive regardless of background.

Our History

A journey of growth and excellence in mathematical education and research.

1992

Department Established

The School of Mathematical Sciences was founded with a vision to provide quality education in pure and applied mathematics, starting with undergraduate programs and a small team of dedicated faculty.

2000

Expansion of Programs

Introduced postgraduate programs in Pure Mathematics, Applied Mathematics, and Statistics to meet the growing demand for advanced mathematical expertise in various sectors.

2008

Research Center Established

Launched a dedicated research center focusing on computational mathematics, mathematical modeling, and interdisciplinary applications, securing significant grants and research collaborations.

2015

National Recognition

Achieved recognition as one of the top mathematics departments in the region, with faculty members receiving prestigious awards and students excelling in national competitions and research initiatives.

Academic Focus Areas

Our department specializes in various branches of mathematics, providing comprehensive education and research opportunities.

Pure Mathematics

Exploring abstract concepts and theoretical foundations including algebra, analysis, topology, and number theory to advance mathematical knowledge.

  • Abstract Algebra
  • Real & Complex Analysis
  • Number Theory

Applied Mathematics

Applying mathematical methods to solve practical problems in science, engineering, economics, and other fields through modeling and analysis.

  • Differential Equations
  • Mathematical Modeling
  • Numerical Analysis

Statistics & Data Science

Developing methods for collecting, analyzing, and interpreting data to extract meaningful insights and support decision-making processes.

  • Statistical Inference
  • Probability Theory
  • Machine Learning
