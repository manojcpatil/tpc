About Our Department

Committed to excellence in education, research, and innovation since 1985.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional education that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values to excel in their chosen fields and make meaningful contributions to society.

We are dedicated to fostering a culture of innovation, critical thinking, and ethical leadership through rigorous academic programs, cutting-edge research, and strong industry partnerships.

Our Vision

To be recognized globally as a center of academic excellence and innovation, producing graduates who are prepared to address complex challenges and drive positive change in a rapidly evolving world.

We aspire to be at the forefront of research and education, creating knowledge that advances human understanding and improves quality of life.

Our History

A journey of growth, innovation, and academic excellence spanning over three decades.

1985

Department Established

Founded with a vision to provide quality education in emerging fields of technology and science, starting with just two programs and 50 students.

1995

Expansion of Programs

Introduced new undergraduate and graduate programs in response to industry needs and technological advancements, doubling student enrollment.

2005

Research Center Established

Launched a dedicated research center focusing on interdisciplinary innovation, securing significant grants and industry partnerships.

2020

Global Recognition

Achieved top rankings in academic excellence, research output, and graduate employability, with a network of alumni making significant contributions worldwide.
