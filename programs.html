Admission Requirements

Information about eligibility criteria and application process for our programs.

Undergraduate Programs

  • Completion of 10+2 or equivalent with Mathematics as a core subject
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks in qualifying examination (45% for reserved categories)
  • Selection based on merit in qualifying examination and/or entrance test conducted by the university

Postgraduate Programs

  • Bachelor's degree in Mathematics or related discipline with minimum 55% aggregate marks (50% for reserved categories)
  • Valid score in university entrance examination or national level tests like JAM/GATE/NET (as applicable)
  • Interview performance for final selection (for some specialized programs)

Doctoral Programs

  • Master's degree in Mathematics or related discipline with minimum 60% aggregate marks (55% for reserved categories)
  • Qualified in UGC-NET/CSIR-NET/GATE or university entrance examination
  • Research proposal and personal interview for final selection
